# Privacy Policy for Tribute

Last updated: July 2026

Tribute (the "App") is a mobile application built to display historical and recent biography details of notable figures. 

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

Tribute does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, user identifier, or location data.

## 2. Third-Party Services

The App retrieves public biography content and images dynamically from Wikipedia's APIs. These requests are sent anonymously. We do not track your searches or send queries to any other third-party servers.

## 3. Local Storage

All settings (such as notification time preferences and category filters) are stored locally on your device and are never sent to any external servers.

## 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

## 5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback, please open an issue on our GitHub support page: https://github.com/beaverslikebuilding/tribute-support/issues
